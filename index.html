About Us

Founded in 2022, ED-Architects is a forward-thinking architectural bureau in Chișinău, Moldova, and Costa Blanca, Spain. We specialize in architectural design, interior solutions, and urban planning, seamlessly blending functionality with contemporary aesthetics.

Our philosophy is rooted in innovation,sustainability, and a deep commitment to understanding our clients vision. From initial concept to final execution, we craft spaces that are not only visually compelling but also practical, efficient, and environmentally conscious.

Backed by a team of skilled architects and designers, ED-Architects delivers precision-driven projects that balance creativity with technical excellence. Whether working on residential, commercial, or urban developments, we are dedicated to shaping inspiring and sustainable environments.

Let’s create the future together. Welcome to ED-Architects.
